The Manifesto

Not Just Content. A Universe.

Faith, imagination, and honest questioning coexist here. Heroes confront real doubts. Fantasy illuminates truth.

Our Philosophy

Truth Does Not Fear Imagination

We believe the next generation doesn't need easier Christianity. They need truer stories — ones that take their doubts seriously, honor their intelligence, and point them toward an encounter they can't manufacture on their own.

Our vision begins with the Abimifoluwa animation: a cinematic journey of faith, curiosity, and courage — crafted to inspire, challenge, and invite the next generation into a world where questions are welcomed and truth shines.

"The light shines in the darkness, and the darkness has not overcome it."
— John 1:5

This is the beginning of something larger. Multiple characters. Multiple worlds. One mission: to help people encounter God through stories that respect both their intelligence and their hearts.
