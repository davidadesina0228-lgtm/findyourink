About Me

David Adesina

I was an atheist. Then an agnostic. Then in 2016, in Ryazan, Russia, I encountered God — and everything changed.

It happened under the ministry of Dr. Samson Ajikobi — then pastoring under Christ Embassy, now founder of Spirit Assembly International (Latvia). He was the one God used to reach me.

From 2018 to 2026 I was discipled under Apostle Nife Ibiyemi, founder of Love Dominion Assembly — years that built the theological backbone behind everything I preach and write.

Today I pastor a church, host Chayah revival meetings — which began in Tbilisi, Georgia with a mandate to go to the ends of the earth — and write for believers who are on fire but breaking on the inside. I am a husband and a father, and I love apologetics, because I have watched honest people lose their faith when no one taught them how to answer the questions that came.

That is why three streams exist: Cave Echoes for revival — Find Your Ink for healing the mind — Gleamshield for the defense of faith. Three fronts. One war. One answer.

The Vision

Three Fronts. One War. One Answer.

The enemy has never attacked on one front. He attacks the fire, the mind, and the reason. This is why revival, healing, and the defense of faith cannot be separated.

Cave Echoes — Revival

The fire has to start somewhere. Revival is not an event — it is a people encountering God and being unable to remain the same. Chayah revival meetings began in Tbilisi, Georgia. The mandate: to the ends of the earth.

Find Your Ink — Healing the Mind

People catch fire for God and then lose it — not because they chose the world, but because unhealed wounds became back doors. These blogs exist to heal what the present-day church has often left unnamed.

Gleamshield — Defense of Faith

In the moment when faith is thin and someone hands you an argument you were never taught to answer, you need more than zeal. Gleamshield equips the next generation with a reason for what they believe.
